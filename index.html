How it works

1) Define ownership & kill-switch

Establish shutdown authority, data boundaries, and escalation paths so control exists during runtime—not in a PDF.

2) Constrain agents with guardrails

Tool allowlists, policy checks, PII redaction, scoped credentials, and budget limits prevent “helpful” mistakes.

3) Observe in-flight behavior

Event logs, traces, and risk scoring provide a real-time picture of what agents did, why, and what happened next.

4) Intervene & improve

Human-in-the-loop approvals, rollback routines, and runbooks turn incidents into upgrades—not outages.
